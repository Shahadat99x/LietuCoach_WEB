# Arrival in Lithuania

Welcome! "Sveiki atvykę!" When you land at VNO (Vilnius Airport) or KUN (Kaunas), here is what to expect language-wise.

## At the Airport (Oro uostas)

- **Pasų kontrolė** — Passport control
- **Atvykimas** — Arrivals
- **Išvykimas** — Departures
- **Bagažas** — Baggage

## Taking a Taxi / Bolt

Apps like Bolt and Uber are dominant. You don't _need_ to speak, but it's polite.

- **Sveiki.** (Hi.)
- **Į centrą, prašom.** (To the center, please.)
- **Čia gerai, ačiū.** (Here is fine, thanks - _when stopping_.)

## At the Hotel (Viešbutis)

- **Turiu rezervaciją.** (I have a reservation.)
- **Kada pusryčiai?** (When is breakfast?)
- **Koks Wi-Fi slaptažodis?** (What is the Wi-Fi password?)

## Emergencies

- **Kur yra tualetas?** (Where is the toilet? - _Vital!_)
- **Vandens, prašom.** (Water, please.)
