# Renting an Apartment

Finding a flat ("Butas") can be stressful. While many landlords speak English, the ads (Skelbiu.lt, Aruodas.lt) are often in Lithuanian.

## Reading the Ads

- **Nuoma** — Rent
- **Butas** — Apartment
- **Kambarys** — Room
- **Ilgalaikė nuoma** — Long-term rent
- **Trumpalaikė nuoma** — Short-term rent
- **Depozitas** — Deposit

## Key Questions for Landlords

- **Kokia kaina mėnesiui?** (What is the price per month?)
- **Ar komunaliniai mokesčiai įskaičiuoti?** (Are utilities included? - _Crucial question!_)
- **Kada galiu apžiūrėti?** (When can I visit/inspect?)

## Utilities (Mokesčiai)

In Lithuania, heating ("šildymas") can be expensive in winter.

- **Šildymas** — Heating
- **Elektra** — Electricity
- **Vanduo** — Water
- **Internetas** — Internet

## Example Message to Landlord

_Sveiki, mane domina butas [Gatvė]. Ar jis dar laisvas? Kada galiu apžiūrėti? Ačiū._
(Hi, I am interested in the flat at [Street]. Is it still free? When can I visit? Thanks.)
