# Essential Verbs

You can't speak without verbs. In Lithuanian, verbs change ending based on "who" is doing it (I, you, he/she).

## 1. Būti (To Be)

The most irregular and important verb.

- **Aš esu** (I am)
- **Tu esi** (You are)
- **Jis/Ji yra** (He/She is)
- Mes esame
- Jūs esate
- Jie/Jos yra

**Example:** _Aš esu studentas._ (I am a student.)

## 2. Turėti (To Have)

- **Aš turiu** (I have)
- **Tu turi** (You have)
- **Jis/Ji turi** (He/She has)

**Example:** _Aš turiu bilietą._ (I have a ticket.)

## 3. Norėti (To Want)

_Requires Genitive case!_

- **Aš noriu** (I want)
- **Tu nori** (You want)
- **Jis/Ji nori** (He/She wants)

**Example:** _Aš noriu vandens._ (I want water.)

## 4. Eiti (To Go - by foot)

- **Aš einu** (I go)
- **Tu eini** (You go)
- **Jis/Ji eina** (He/She goes)

**Example:** _Aš einu namo._ (I go home.)

## Common Mistake

Don't say "Aš yra". Always match the person.

- Correct: _Aš esu._
- Wrong: _Aš yra._
