# University Life in Lithuania

Vilnius and Kaunas are student cities. Whether you are at VU, VGTU, or KTU, these phrases will help you navigate campus life.

## Campus Vocabulary

- **Universitetas** — University
- **Paskaita** — Lecture
- **Bendrabutis** (or just "Bendrabutis") — Dormitory
- **Biblioteka** — Library
- **Valgykla** — Canteen (Cheap food!)

## Making Friends

- **Ką studijuoji?** (What do you study?)
- **Aš studijuoju [programą]** (I study...)
  - _Informatiką_ (IT)
  - _Mediciną_ (Medicine)
  - _Vadybą_ (Management)
- **Ar eini į vakarėlį?** (Are you going to the party?)

## In the Dorms

- **Tyliau, prašom.** (Quiet, please.)
- **Nėra interneto.** (There is no internet.)
- **Kur yra skalbykla?** (Where is the laundry room?)

## Example Dialogue: The Canteen

**You:** _Sveiki, norėčiau cepelinų._ (Hi, I would like cepelinai.)
**Staff:** _Vieną ar du?_ (One or two?)
**You:** _Du, prašom. Ir grietinės._ (Two, please. And sour cream.)
**Staff:** _Mokėsite kortele?_ (Will you pay by card?)
**You:** _Taip._ (Yes.)

## Mini FAQ

### Do professors speak English?

Yes, most younger professors and almost all details for international students are in English. But knowing "Ačiū už paskaitą" (Thanks for the lecture) goes a long way.

### What is "Erasmus"?

You probably know this! But locally, "Erasmus" often refers to the parties or the students themselves.
